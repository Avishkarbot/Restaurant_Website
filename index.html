Chef / Owner Avishkar Yelave began baking pies and selling them to resturant and his neighbours out of small kitchen at the corner of Husdon and North Moore St. in Tribeca. Today, NYC'c beloved restuarant and pie shop celebrates 27 years of classic, made from scratch Indian cooking
